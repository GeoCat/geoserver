GeoServer Enterprise
====================

Welcome to :command:`GeoServer Enterprise` |version| distribution provided by `GeoCat BV <https://www.geocat.net/>`__.

We are pleased to offer :command:`GeoServer Enterprise Standard` with long-term-support up to three years with a selection of the most popular extensions as requested by our customers. For more demanding environments :command:`GeoServer Enterprise Premium` offers extended-support up to five years, a greater selection of extensions.
